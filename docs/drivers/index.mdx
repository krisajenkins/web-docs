# Overview of drivers

The list of drivers available in the [Toit library](https://libs.toit.io/drivers/library-summary) can be viewed here:


*Driver* | *Usage* | *Link*
  --- | ---
  `bma2x2` | Driver for Bosch accelerometers |
  `bme280` | Driver for BME280 environmental sensor |
  `bmg160` | Driver for the gyroscope BMG160 |
  `bmm050` | Driver for the magnometer BMG160 |
  `sara_r4` | Driver for Sara-R4, GSM communicating over NB-IoT & M. |
  `sara_r5` | Driver for Sara-R5, GSM communicating over NB-IoT & M1 |
  `ublox_cellular` | Base driver for u-blox Cellular devices, communicating over CAT-NB1 and/or CAT-M1 |
